Prague, Czech Republic

Dear reader,

Oh, Prague! A city that looks like it was designed by a romantic novelist and an architect having coffee together. Every alley has a story, and every tower waves at the sky.

But beware! Beneath that dreamy skyline, the cobblestones whisper, “Watch your wallet.” We were serenaded by overpriced menus, and even the pigeons seemed suspiciously organized. Still, I’d go back... but maybe with a money belt.

With whimsy and wisdom,
— Me

Vienna, Austria

Dear reader,

Vienna was like walking through a classical symphony—graceful, regal, and full of history. From the grandeur of Schönbrunn Palace to the melodic bustle of street performers, every step resonated with culture. Yet beneath its beauty, we found the metro system less secure than expected, particularly for tourists. Vigilance was essential amidst the city's charm.

With memories and caution,
— Me

Dresden, Germany

Dear reader,

Dresden was a canvas of resilience, art, and architectural splendor. Its reconstructed heart bore the echoes of a complex past. While its historic core felt safe and welcoming, we were advised against venturing into less lit outskirts at night. A beautiful city with cautionary tales lingering at its edges.

Reflectively,
— Me
